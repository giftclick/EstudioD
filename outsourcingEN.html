Outsourcing

At ARNEZ MIRANDA Y ASOCIADOS, we aim to meet the information and control needs of companies that do not have the structure to develop these activities.

The outsourcing services we provide to companies include:

  • Processing the company's information and issuing the required financial information.
  • Internal audits by conducting reviews according to a work plan designed for this purpose.
  • Accounting Outsourcing
  • Internal Audit Outsourcing

Accounting Outsourcing

Our accounting outsourcing includes:

  • Registration with Tax Authorities, Corporations, Pension Funds, Ministry of Labor, and other entities required by law.
  • Collection and recording of accounting information.
  • Issuance of financial statements.
  • Payroll processing.
  • Preparation and submission of monthly and annual tax returns, labor declarations, and other social security requirements.
  • Physical inventory taking.
  • Compilation of financial statements.

Internal Audit Outsourcing

We offer internal audit outsourcing:

  • Compliance audit of internal controls.
  • Compliance audit of internal administrative procedures.
